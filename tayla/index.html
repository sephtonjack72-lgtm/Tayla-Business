How it works

Standing orders let you set up recurring purchase templates per supplier — weekly, fortnightly, or monthly.

On the scheduled date, a draft PO is automatically generated and flagged for review. You can edit quantities before sending.

Standing orders are separate from suggested orders — they run on a fixed schedule regardless of stock levels.
